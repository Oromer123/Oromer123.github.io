But first who is Ralsei ?

Ralsei is a character in the Game "Deltarune". He is part of the main protagonist group and friends with the characters Susie and Kris. He has a whole bunch of other friends on the account of him being nice and wholesome but those are the main two.

Many people of the fandom like Ralsei, some more than others. There is a large amount of fanart and communites that have formed around this singular love for the cute goat.
